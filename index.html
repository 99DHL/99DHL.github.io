Curriculum Vitae

Hello! I am a 4th year Ph.D student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Seoul National University. I am a member of Architecture and Code Optimization (ARC) Lab led by Professor Jae W. Lee.

My research focuses on systems for machine learning, with current interests in efficient 3D vision and GPU kernel optimization.

I received my B.S. degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Yonsei University in 2022.
